How It Works

Up and running in minutes

No complex setup. Register, get your certificate, and authenticate immediately.

01

Register

Submit your username and email. VaultKey generates a 2048-bit RSA key pair and issues your X.509 certificate from the Root CA.

02

Save your keys

Download and securely store your private key PEM and certificate. These are your credentials — guard them like a password.

03

Login

Paste your certificate PEM to authenticate. VaultKey validates the signature and issues a time-limited JWT session token.

04

Use the platform

Store encrypted credentials in your vault, sign documents, or manage certificates — all backed by your PKI identity.
